The Best Movies According to Bari

Here are my top 3 movies of all time.

THE GODFATHER I & II

A real-life story of The Italian Don (Don Corleone), His growth as a don and his demise and there are life in th 19th century of USA.

3 MOONU

A beautiful love story of a school boy, and how he fell in love for a girl and the life story. The movie has a lot of great twist and turn and it probably has one of the best twisted climax. worth a watch.

IN-TIME

A mind-bending story of a world with time is the currency of life. I mean everything! It show's how time is used for everything you want in your life and makes us realise the value of time.
